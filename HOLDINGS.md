## 📈 Trading Strategy & Rules

### Position Sizing
- **Max position size**: 15% of total portfolio per stock
- **Min position size**: 5% to ensure meaningful impact
- **Target number of positions**: 3-5 stocks (diversification without over-diversification)

### Risk Management
- **Stop Loss**: 2% below entry price (automatic mental stop)
- **Max Daily Loss**: 5% of total portfolio
- **Max Drawdown**: 10% before review needed

### Entry/Exit Rules
- **Entry signals required**:
  - Technical confirmation (RSI, MACD, or breakout)
  - Positive news sentiment OR strong fundamentals
  - Volume confirmation (above average)

- **Exit signals**:
  - Stop loss hit (2% loss)
  - Target hit (typically 5-10% gain)
  - Negative news affecting outlook
  - Technical breakdown (support break)

### Trading Hours
- **Preferred**: First 30 min (9:30-10:00 AM EST) and last hour (3:00-4:00 PM EST)
- **Avoid**: Lunch time (12:00-2:00 PM EST) - lower volume
